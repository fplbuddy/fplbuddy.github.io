FPL Buddy


Here are some numbers which will help you beat your mates in Fantasy Premier League. The Expected Goals Scored are exactly what you expect. The number of goals we expect each team to score in any given gameweek. The Defensive Score is a little funky. It is proportional to the probability that each team holds a clean sheet. Note that both of these statistics will get a boost in double gameweeks, so look out for that! Click on teams in the table to get additional team specific data.


The data is calculated using the Dixon Coles Model.
