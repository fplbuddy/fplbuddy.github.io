	<h2> FPL Helper </h2>

    <p> Here are some numbers which will help you beat your mates in Fantasy Premier League. The <b> Expected Goals Scored </b> are exactly what you expect. The number of goals we expect each team to score in any given gameweek. The <b> Defensive Score </b> is a little funky. It is proportional to the probability that each team holds a clean sheet.
